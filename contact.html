Abstract submission

Please send titles and abstracts for contributed talks to CS21.winds@gmail.com. We will send a confirmation that we have received your abstract within a few working days.

We also plan to hold a series of rapid fire talks where each person will get 1 slide and a short amount of time to promote their poster/work. If you would like to give a 1 slide talk, please send us a title (no abstract required). Abstracts which are not selected to give a talk will be automatically converted to a 1 slide talk. Please indicate in your email if you are submitting for a full talk or rapid talk.

The abstract submission deadline is 13th May 2022. You will be informed if your talk has been selected shortly after this date.
